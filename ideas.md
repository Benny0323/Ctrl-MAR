# Ctrl-MAR Project Page — Design Ideas

## Approach A: Scientific Monograph
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets medical imaging aesthetics
**Core Principles:** Grid-based precision, typographic hierarchy, clinical restraint, data-forward
**Color Philosophy:** Near-white background (#FAFAFA), deep navy (#0F1B2D) text, electric teal (#00B4D8) accent — evoking medical monitors and scientific rigor
**Layout Paradigm:** Strict column grid, left-aligned text blocks, full-bleed section dividers
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rules, monospace labels, CT scan-inspired grayscale motifs
**Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal animation, content-first, keyboard-friendly
**Animation:** Subtle fade-in on scroll, no decorative motion
**Typography System:** IBM Plex Serif (headings) + IBM Plex Sans (body) — clinical authority

## Approach B: Dark Lab Aesthetic
**Design Movement:** Brutalist-Scientific Dark Mode — think CERN dashboards
**Core Principles:** High contrast, raw data visualization feel, asymmetric tension, bold type
**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#0D0D0D) background, pure white text, amber (#F59E0B) highlights — like oscilloscope traces
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column with wide left gutters, full-width image strips
**Signature Elements:** Glowing borders on key figures, scanline texture overlays, monospace everywhere
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover reveals, parallax CT scan backgrounds
**Animation:** Glitch-free but dramatic entrance animations, section wipes
**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (display) + JetBrains Mono (labels) — hacker-lab energy
<probability>0.05</probability>

## Approach C: Academic Elegance — CVPR/NeurIPS Style
**Design Movement:** Contemporary academic paper aesthetics — clean, authoritative, readable
**Core Principles:** Generous whitespace, typographic hierarchy, figure-centric layout, accessibility
**Color Philosophy:** Warm white (#FFFDF9) background, charcoal (#1A1A2E) text, deep blue (#1D4ED8) primary, soft amber (#F59E0B) accent — trustworthy and scholarly
**Layout Paradigm:** Centered single-column with max-width constraint, section anchors, sticky nav
**Signature Elements:** Paper-like section cards, citation badges, comparison figure grids
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll, image lightbox, tab-based result comparisons
**Animation:** Elegant fade-up on scroll, smooth section transitions
**Typography System:** Playfair Display (title) + Source Serif 4 (body) + JetBrains Mono (code/labels)
<probability>0.08</probability>

---

**Selected Approach: C — Academic Elegance (CVPR/NeurIPS Style)**

This is the most appropriate for a research project page targeting the computer vision community. The warm white background and serif typography evoke a printed academic paper, while the blue accent and amber highlights provide visual hierarchy without distracting from the scientific content. The centered layout with generous whitespace ensures figures and equations are the focal point.
